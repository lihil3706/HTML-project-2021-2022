Background:

Paris is the capital and the most popular city in France.Since the 17th century, Paris has been one of Europe's major centres of finance, diplomacy, commerce, fashion, gastronomy, science, and arts. Paris is a first-rate cultural center. It is a tourist destination for about 26 million foreign tourists each year, operates over 210 museums, including the Louvre, and unique sites, including the Champs Elysees and the Eiffel Tower. Paris is the capital of business, fashion, luxury, gastronomy and love, and excels in a wide variety of theater performances , Opera and most importantly, a rare supply of films from all over the world.
